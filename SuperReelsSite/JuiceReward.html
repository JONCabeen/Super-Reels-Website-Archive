Juice Reward

Juice Reward is a dark surrealist comedy, a satirical parody of YouTuber "Mumkey Jones" aka "Simian Jimmy," and a follow up to the equally strange Mumkey Jones Gets Facially Hugged. The story behind why the film exists in the first place, and understanding all of the references in the film, makes it very complicated to explain to people outside of it's very niche audience. Despite that, or perhaps because of that, it is one of the films we look back on with utmost fondness, both in terms of the experience making it and the end result. It was our first film shot on Ben's Black Magic 4K Cinema camera with a cinematic lense, so it marks the beginning of a step up in quality for us, which raised our standards for films going forwards.
