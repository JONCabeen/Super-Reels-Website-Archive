What Little Remains

What Little Remains was made for another Simian Jimmy short film contest. Jonathan wrote this story with the neo-noir genre on his mind, thinking of movies like Blade Runner 2049 and Ghost in the Shell. The film follows a detective who uses futuristic technology to solve his cases facing an early retirement after a life-altering traumatic event. He solves one last case on his final night as a detective.
